# Municipal Road & Civil Infrastructure Upgrade (Phase 2)

**Tender Reference:** T06/2026/GPT
**Issuing Authority:** City of Johannesburg - Group Planning & Transport
**Sector:** Infrastructure — Road & Civil Works

## Project Description
The project involves the upgrading of approximately 8.5 km of municipal roads in the Sandton area, including complete reconstruction of road pavement layers, stormwater drainage improvements, street lighting, and associated civil works.

## Key Details
- **Location:** Sandton, Johannesburg, Gauteng
- **Estimated Duration:** 24 months
- **Budget Range:** ZAR 35-45 million
- **CIDB Grading Required:** 7 CE or higher

## Scope of Work
1. Site clearing and preparation
2. Bulk earthworks including cut and fill
3. Concrete foundations (grade 30MPa)
4. Reinforcement steel installation
5. Road base layer construction (G5 material)
6. Asphalt wearing course (40mm)
7. Stormwater drainage system
8. Water supply piping (HDPE DN200)
9. Street lighting installation
10. Traffic signage and road markings
