# Tender Engine AI — Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** May 2026

---

## 1. Introduction

Tender Engine AI ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information in accordance with the Protection of Personal Information Act, 2013 ("POPIA") of the Republic of South Africa.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, process, and disclose your personal information when you use our Service. It also describes your rights under POPIA and how you can exercise them.

## 2. What Data We Collect

### 2.1 Uploaded Tender Documents
When you use Tender Engine AI, you may upload tender documents (PDF, Word, Excel, or other formats) for processing and analysis. These documents may contain personal information including but not limited to names, contact details, company information, and financial data.

**Purpose:** To provide tender document analysis, extraction, and processing services.

### 2.2 Authentication and Account Data
When you register for an account, we collect:

- Email address
- Full name (optional)
- Password (stored as a securely hashed value — we never store raw passwords)
- Account creation timestamp

**Purpose:** To manage your account, authenticate your access, and communicate with you about your account.

### 2.3 Usage Analytics
We automatically collect certain information when you use the Service, including:

- Pages visited and features used
- Processing requests and their outcomes
- Browser type and version
- Device type and operating system
- Date and time of access
- Referring website or application

**Purpose:** To improve the Service, diagnose technical issues, and understand usage patterns.

### 2.4 Cookies and Local Storage
We use cookies and local browser storage for:

- Session management (authentication tokens)
- User preferences and settings
- Active job tracking (localStorage stores only job IDs — no personal data)

**Purpose:** To maintain your session, remember your preferences, and enable seamless Service operation.

## 3. How We Process Data

We process your personal information for the following purposes:

- **Service Delivery:** To process uploaded tender documents and provide extraction, analysis, and reporting results
- **Account Management:** To create and maintain your account, provide customer support, and communicate service-related information
- **Service Improvement:** To analyse usage patterns, improve processing accuracy, and enhance the user experience
- **Security:** To detect, prevent, and respond to security incidents, fraud, and abuse
- **Legal Compliance:** To comply with applicable legal obligations under South African law

## 4. POPIA Compliance

We process personal information in compliance with the Protection of Personal Information Act, 2013 (POPIA). This means we:

- Process personal information lawfully, fairly, and transparently
- Collect personal information only for specified, explicit, and legitimate purposes
- Limit collection to what is adequate, relevant, and not excessive
- Ensure personal information is accurate and kept up to date
- Retain personal information no longer than necessary
- Protect personal information with appropriate security measures
- Respect your rights as a data subject under POPIA

## 5. Lawful Basis for Processing

Under POPIA, we process your personal information on the following lawful bases:

- **Consent:** You have given clear consent for us to process your personal information for a specific purpose (e.g., account registration, document processing)
- **Contractual Necessity:** Processing is necessary for the performance of a contract with you (our Terms of Service)
- **Legal Obligation:** Processing is necessary for compliance with a legal obligation to which we are subject
- **Legitimate Interest:** Processing is necessary for our legitimate interests (e.g., Service improvement, security) provided these do not override your rights and interests

## 6. Consent

By using the Service and accepting this Privacy Policy, you provide your consent to the collection, processing, and storage of your personal information as described herein. You have the right to withdraw your consent at any time by contacting us or deleting your account.

**Withdrawal of consent does not affect the lawfulness of processing based on consent before its withdrawal.**

## 7. Your Rights Under POPIA

As a data subject under POPIA, you have the following rights:

### 7.1 Right to Access
You have the right to request confirmation of whether we hold personal information about you and to request access to that information. We will provide a copy of your personal information in our possession within a reasonable timeframe.

### 7.2 Right to Correction
You have the right to request the correction of inaccurate, incomplete, or misleading personal information we hold about you. You can update most account information directly through your account settings.

### 7.3 Right to Deletion
You have the right to request the deletion of your personal information where:
- The information is no longer needed for the purpose for which it was collected
- You withdraw consent and no other lawful basis for processing exists
- The information was processed unlawfully
- Deletion is required to comply with a legal obligation

### 7.4 Right to Object
You have the right to object to the processing of your personal information on grounds relating to your particular situation, where processing is based on legitimate interest.

### 7.5 Right to Restrict Processing
You have the right to request restriction of processing where:
- You contest the accuracy of the information
- Processing is unlawful and you oppose deletion
- We no longer need the information but you require it for legal claims
- You have objected to processing and verification is pending

### 7.6 Right to Data Portability
You have the right to receive your personal information in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format and to transmit that information to another controller where processing is based on consent or contract and is carried out by automated means.

## 8. Exercising Your Rights

To exercise any of your rights under POPIA, please contact us using the information in the Contact section below. We will respond to your request within a reasonable timeframe and in accordance with POPIA requirements.

We may need to verify your identity before processing your request. We will not charge a fee for exercising your rights unless the request is manifestly unfounded, excessive, or repetitive.

## 9. Data Retention Periods

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes for which it was collected:

| Data Type | Retention Period |
|-----------|-----------------|
| Account information | Duration of active account + 90 days after deletion |
| Uploaded documents | Duration of active account + 90 days after deletion |
| Processing results | Duration of active account + 90 days after deletion |
| Usage analytics | 12 months (anonymised thereafter) |
| Authentication tokens | Duration of session |
| Local storage data | Cleared on logout or browser session end |

Where processing logs are retained for service improvement, they are anonymised and cannot be linked to a specific individual.

## 10. Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organisational security measures to protect your personal information against:

- Unauthorised access, disclosure, or use
- Accidental or unlawful destruction
- Loss or alteration
- Unlawful processing

Security measures include, but are not limited to:

- **Encryption:** All data in transit is encrypted using TLS. Passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms.
- **Access Control:** Access to personal information is restricted to authorised personnel only, on a need-to-know basis.
- **Infrastructure Security:** Our hosting infrastructure includes firewalls, intrusion detection, and regular security updates.
- **Secure Development:** We follow secure coding practices and conduct regular security reviews.

**No security system is impenetrable. We cannot guarantee the absolute security of your data.**

## 11. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party service providers to assist in delivering the Service, including:

- **Cloud hosting providers** — for infrastructure and data storage
- **AI/ML service providers** — for document processing and analysis
- **Analytics services** — for usage monitoring and improvement

Where third parties process your personal information on our behalf, we ensure they are contractually bound to:

## 12. International Processing Disclosure

Your personal information may be processed outside the Republic of South Africa, including through AI/ML service providers whose servers may be located in other jurisdictions.

Where personal information is transferred across international borders, we take steps to ensure that:

- The recipient is subject to a law, binding corporate rules, or agreement which provides an adequate level of protection substantially similar to POPIA
- Where required, your consent has been obtained for such transfer

## 13. Storage in South Africa

Where reasonably possible and technically feasible, your personal information and uploaded documents are stored on servers located within the Republic of South Africa. Some processing operations, particularly those involving AI/ML analysis, may involve service providers operating outside South Africa as described in Section 12 above.
